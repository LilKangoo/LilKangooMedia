Gentle Educational Notes

When should I bring my bouquet?

As soon as it feels right. Fresh bouquets are welcome, and dried ones are always possible too.

Can I use a dried bouquet?

Yes. Dried bouquets carry beautiful textures and soft tones that age gracefully.

If my bouquet is years old?

It may still be possible. Send a photo and I will guide you with care.

Use these as Stories during the month for trust and guidance.

Seasonal & Social Proof

Nov–Dec in Cyprus has fewer weddings. It’s an ideal time for dried bouquets and older keepsakes.

Create a Story Highlight: “Words from the heart” with client messages like “Thank you for preserving our moment.”
