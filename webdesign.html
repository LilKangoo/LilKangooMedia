FAQs

Check the most asked question about our services.

Q: What makes a website successful?

A: A combination of great design and user experience.

Q: How do you approach the website design process?

A: Our approach to website design involves understanding your business goals & target audience. We will work with you to create a design that aligns with your brand identity & messaging while also considering user experience & website functionality. We prioritise creating a website that is both visually appealing & easy to use.

Q: How do you ensure the website is optimised for search engines?

A: To optimise a website for search engines, we ensure that the website has a clear structure, uses relevant keywords & includes high-quality content. We also make sure that the website is technically sound, meaning it has fast loading times, is mobile-friendly & has clean code. Additionally, we will set up Google Analytics and Google Search Console to track website traffic & performance.

Q: How do you handle website maintenance and updates?

A: We offer ongoing website maintenance & updates to ensure your website remains secure, up-to-date & functioning properly. This includes regular backups, software updates, security monitoring & troubleshooting any issues that may arise. We can also provide content updates & other changes as needed.

Q: How do you ensure the website is accessible to all users?

A: We ensure that the website is accessible to all users by following accessibility standards & guidelines, such as the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). This includes using alt text for images, providing keyboard navigation, ensuring colour contrast is appropriate & using headings and subheadings to organise content. We also test the website on different devices & assistive technologies to ensure accessibility.

Q: What types of websites have you designed and developed in the past?

A: We have designed & developed a wide range of websites in the past, including e-commerce sites, corporate websites, educational websites & more. We have experience working with clients from various industries & with different needs and goals.
