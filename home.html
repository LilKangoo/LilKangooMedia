Michael Ben Gour

Photographer & Videographer

Michael is a published photographer that has been working for almost a decade. His work can be seen in magazines, newspapers in various countries and all over social media.

Although his work is mainly focused on creating unique portraits of people, he loves capturing beautiful landscapes and he is experienced in product photography for advertising purposes.

Lilyanna

Graphic Designer & Writer

Lily is multitalented artist who specialises in graphic design, illustrations, writing. She studied in Athens and has worked all around Europe.

Her work can be seen in many album covers for music bands, posters, hand-drawn illustrations and business cards. Her main intention is to help businesses look attractive and to present themselves in the highest of standards. Her art work was collected by Sir Anthony Hopkins!
