Larnaca

6 Steps - day in Paphos

Paphos

City in the Republic of Cyprus, in the Paphos District. In 2011, it had a population of 32,892.

It is situated on the southwestern coast of Cyprus. An important tourist centre. The country's fourth largest city.

6 Steps - day in Ayia Napa

Ayia Napa

City in the Famagusta district of Cyprus. It has a population of 3,212 according to 2011 census data.

To the east of the village is Kawo Greko promontory, which is the most southeastern part of Cyprus. It is protected as Kavo Gkreko National Forest Park and Natura 2000 area.
