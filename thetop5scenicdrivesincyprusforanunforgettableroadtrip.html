The Top 5 Scenic Drives in Cyprus for an Unforgettable Road Trip

Cyprus, the Mediterranean's third-largest island, is a stunning destination that offers a perfect blend of history, culture & natural beauty. If you're planning a trip to Cyprus, a road trip is an excellent way to explore this picturesque island. From rugged coastlines to winding mountain roads, Cyprus has it all. In this article we'll take you on a journey to discover the top 5 scenic drives in Cyprus for an unforgettable road trip.

Coastal Road from Larnaca to Ayia Napa

The coastal road from Larnaca to Ayia Napa is a breathtaking drive that offers stunning views of the Mediterranean sea. As you drive along this scenic route, you'll be able to see the crystal clear waters & white sandy beaches of Cyprus. Stop along the way to take a dip in the sea or enjoy a picnic on the beach.

Troodos Mountains

The Troodos Mountains are a must-see for anyone visiting Cyprus. The winding roads offer spectacular views of the mountains, valleys & forests. Stop along the way to visit traditional Cypriot villages, taste local cuisine & explore historic churches and monasteries.

Akamas Peninsula

The Akamas Peninsula, located on the western coast of Cyprus, is a nature lover's paradise. The rugged landscape offers stunning views of the sea, cliffs & gorges. Drive through the Akamas National Park & stop at various lookout points for breathtaking views.

Nicosia to Kyrenia

The drive from Nicosia to Kyrenia takes you through some of the most beautiful countrysides in Cyprus. As you drive along this scenic route, you'll be able to see the beautiful coastline, rolling hills & charming villages. Stop along the way to explore the castles & monasteries that dot the landscape.

Limassol to Paphos

The coastal drive from Limassol to Paphos is one of the most picturesque routes in Cyprus. The road winds along the coastline, offering stunning views of the sea and the rugged landscape. Stop along the way to explore the ancient ruins of Kourion & the famous Aphrodite's Rock.

Frequently asked questions

  • Q. What is the best time to visit Cyprus for a road trip?
  • A. The best time to visit Cyprus for a road trip is in the spring (March to May) or autumn (September to November) when the weather is pleasant & the crowds are thinner.
  • Q. Do I need a special license to drive in Cyprus?
  • A. No, you can drive in Cyprus with your regular driver's license as long as it's in English.
  • Q. Are there any toll roads in Cyprus?
  • A. No, there are no toll roads in Cyprus.
