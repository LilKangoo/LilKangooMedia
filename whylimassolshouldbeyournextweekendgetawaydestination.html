Why Limassol Should Be Your Next Weekend Getaway Destination

If you're looking for a quick weekend getaway that offers a perfect mix of sun, sea & culture, then Limassol should be your top choice. This vibrant coastal city on the southern coast of Cyprus is an ideal destination for a short break. Limassol has something for everyone, from ancient ruins & historical landmarks to beautiful beaches & delicious cuisine. In this article we'll explore why Limassol should be your next weekend getaway destination.

Rich History

Limassol is one of the oldest cities in Cyprus with a rich history dating back to the Bronze Age. The city has a wealth of historical landmarks & ancient ruins that are worth exploring. Some of the top historical attractions in Limassol include:

- Limassol Castle: This medieval castle was built in the 13th century & now houses the Cyprus Medieval Museum.
- Ancient Kourion: These ruins date back to the 2nd century BC & include an ancient theater, baths, and villas.
- Kolossi Castle: This castle was built in the 15th century & was once a center for the production of Commandaria wine.

Stunning Beaches

Limassol is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in Cyprus. Whether you're looking for a quiet spot to relax or a lively beach to party Limassol has it all. Some of the top beaches in Limassol include:

Lady's Mile Beach: This 7km-long beach is one of the most popular in Limassol, with crystal-clear waters & plenty of water sports activities.
Governor's Beach: This secluded beach is perfect for those looking for a quiet spot to relax & enjoy the sun.
Dasoudi Beach: This lively beach is popular with locals & tourists alike, with plenty of bars, restaurants & beach clubs.

Delicious Cuisine

One of the highlights of any trip to Limassol is its delicious cuisine. The city has a vibrant food scene that combines traditional Cypriot dishes with international flavors. Some of the must-try dishes in Limassol include:

Meze: This is a traditional Cypriot meal that consists of a variety of small dishes, such as grilled halloumi cheese, tzatziki & kleftiko (slow-cooked lamb).
Souvlaki: This is a popular Greek dish that consists of grilled meat (usually pork) served on a skewer with vegetables & pita bread.
Loukoumades: These are small, sweet doughnuts that are deep-fried & drizzled with honey & cinnamon.

Exciting Nightlife

Limassol is also known for its exciting nightlife, with plenty of bars, clubs & restaurants to choose from. Whether you're looking for a quiet drink or a wild night out, Limassol has something for everyone. Some of the top nightlife spots in Limassol include:

Saripolou Square: This lively square is home to some of the best bars & clubs in Limassol.
Old Port: This renovated port area has become a popular nightlife spot, with plenty of bars & restaurants overlooking the sea.
Columbia Plaza: This shopping mall & entertainment complex has several bars & restaurants that stay open late.

Frequently asked questions

  • Q: How do I get to Limassol?
  • A: You can fly into Larnaca or Paphos airports & then take a bus or taxi to Limassol. Alternatively, you can also hire a car and drive down to the city.
  • Q: What is the best time to visit Limassol?
  • A: The best time to visit Limassol is between April and November when the weather is warm and sunny. The peak tourist season is during July and August, so if you want to avoid the crowds it's best to visit during the shoulder season.
  • Q: What are some other attractions in Limassol?
  • A: In addition to its beaches, historical landmarks & nightlife, Limassol also has several other attractions worth visiting. These include the Limassol Zoo, the Limassol Marina & the Limassol Sculpture Park.

Limassol is the perfect destination for a weekend getaway. With its rich history, stunning beaches, delicious cuisine & exciting nightlife, there's something for everyone in this vibrant coastal city. Whether you're looking to explore ancient ruins, relax on the beach, or party the night away, Limassol has it all. So why not book your next weekend getaway to Limassol today and experience all that this amazing city has to offer!
